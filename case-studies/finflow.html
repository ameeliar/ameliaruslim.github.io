FinFlow - Banking Reimagined

Redesigning the mobile banking experience for Gen Z users

Role

Lead Product Designer

Team

2 Designers, 4 Engineers, 1 PM

Timeline

4 months

Impact

40% increase in DAU

The Challenge

FinFlow, a digital-first bank, was struggling to engage their Gen Z user base. Despite competitive rates and features, their mobile app felt dated and didn't resonate with younger users who expected modern, intuitive experiences.

The company approached us to completely reimagine their mobile banking experience while maintaining trust and security - critical factors in financial products.

Key Problem Statement

How might we create a mobile banking experience that feels fresh and engaging for Gen Z users while maintaining the trust and security they expect from a financial institution?

Research & Discovery

We began with extensive user research to understand the needs, behaviors, and pain points of our target audience.

Research Methods

  • 20+ user interviews with Gen Z banking customers
  • Competitive analysis of 15 fintech apps
  • Survey of 500+ respondents aged 18-27
  • Diary studies tracking banking behaviors over 2 weeks

Key Insights

01

Visual Trust

Users associated modern, polished UI with security and reliability - contrary to traditional banking assumptions.

02

Micro-moments

Most banking interactions were quick balance checks and transfers, yet these took too many taps.

03

Financial Anxiety

Users wanted insights into their spending without feeling judged or overwhelmed.

Design Process

Information Architecture

We restructured the app around the most common user tasks, reducing the average task completion from 6 taps to 2.

IA Diagram

Visual Design

We developed a fresh visual language that felt modern yet trustworthy, using a combination of bold gradients, clean typography, and thoughtful micro-interactions.

Visual Design System

Key Screens

Home Dashboard

Personalized dashboard showing balance, recent activity, and quick actions

Insights

Friendly spending insights without judgment

Transfer

Simplified transfer flow with smart suggestions

Outcomes & Impact

The redesigned app launched in Q3 2024 and exceeded all success metrics.

40% Increase in Daily Active Users
65% Improvement in Task Completion
4.8 App Store Rating (up from 3.2)
52% Increase in Feature Adoption

Reflections

This project reinforced the importance of challenging assumptions. Traditional banking design principles didn't resonate with our audience - they wanted modern experiences that felt native to the apps they use daily.

The biggest learning was finding the balance between innovation and trust. We pushed boundaries in visual design while ensuring core banking interactions remained familiar and secure.
